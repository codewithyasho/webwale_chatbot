Legal content
Eligibility window
- Refunds may be requested before work begins or within 48 hours of kickoff.
- Once production starts, refunds are evaluated by milestone.
- Completed work is billable and non-refundable except as stated under client cancellations.

Deposits and milestones
- Deposits reserve project time and are non-refundable.
- The token amount is not refundable at any cost.
- Milestone payments cover work completed up to that stage.
- Any eligible refund excludes third-party fees.

Client cancellations
- Notify us in writing to cancel a project.
- We will invoice for completed work before cancellation.
- If the project is canceled after it is done or ready, we keep 50% of the total amount plus the token amount, and refund the remaining balance.
- Unused project time may be credited to a future booking.

Refund process
- Email us with your project name and refund request.
- We will confirm eligibility within 5 business days.
- Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method.

Chargebacks
- Please contact us before filing a chargeback.
- Chargebacks pause work and may delay future support.
- We provide documentation for completed milestones.
